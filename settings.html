📱 Add ReelCall to Your Home Screen

ReelCall works like a native app — add it to your home screen for one-tap access.

🍎 iPhone (Safari)

  1. Open reelcall.app/reel.html in Safari
  2. Tap the Share icon (square with up arrow) at the bottom
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
  4. Tap Add

🌍 Android (Chrome)

  1. Open reelcall.app/reel.html in Chrome
  2. Tap the 3-dot menu (top right)
  3. Tap Add to Home screen or Install app
  4. Tap Add / Install
